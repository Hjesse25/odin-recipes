Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • 2 cups Flour
  • 1 ¾ cups milk
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 4 teaspoons baking powder
  • Baking powder
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • nonstick cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat a waffle iron
  2. Whisk eggs in a large bowl. Add flour, milk, and vegetable oil and mix together. Whisk in suger, then baking powder, salt and vanilla just until smooth.
  3. Spray the waffle iron with cooking spray. Pour batter on it and cook until golden brown 3-5 minutes.
